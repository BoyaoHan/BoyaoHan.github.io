Research Interests
======

### Embodied AI

My research focuses on embodied artificial intelligence, with an emphasis on improving the **generalization of Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models** in real-world environments.

I am particularly interested in the role of **scaling laws** in embodied agents — how large-scale real-world data can be leveraged to enhance the robustness and generalization of policies across diverse tasks and environments. I also believe that VLA models relying solely on RGB input struggle to handle the complexity of real-world interaction; incorporating physical signals such as **depth and tactile feedback** is essential for modeling environmental structure and enabling fine-grained manipulation.

### Computer Vision

In computer vision, I focus on the application of **3D Computer Vision** to embodied intelligence, including how to build structured 3D representations of the environment, improve spatial understanding, and combine multimodal information for scene modeling and decision making.

I am also interested in **World Models**: by learning the dynamics and structure of the environment, world models can provide embodied agents with stronger predictive capability — a foundation for higher-level **spatial intelligence**.
